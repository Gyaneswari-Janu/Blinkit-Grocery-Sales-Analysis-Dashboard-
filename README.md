# 🛒 BlinkIT Grocery Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **BlinkIT Grocery Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence project developed using **Power BI** to analyze grocery sales performance across different outlet types, outlet sizes, product categories, and geographical locations.

This dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and dynamic filtering. It enables stakeholders to identify top-performing products, evaluate outlet performance, monitor customer satisfaction, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem Statement

BlinkIT operates through multiple outlets and sells thousands of products across different locations. With large volumes of transactional data being generated daily, it becomes difficult to manually analyze sales trends, product performance, and outlet efficiency.

The objective of this project is to convert raw sales data into actionable insights that help management:

* Identify high-performing product categories.
* Understand outlet performance across locations.
* Monitor customer ratings and satisfaction.
* Optimize inventory and sales strategies.
* Support business expansion decisions.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Product Performance

* Fruits & Vegetables generated the highest sales revenue.
* Snack Foods ranked as the second-best performing category.
* Household products maintained consistent sales performance.

### Location Analysis

* Tier 3 locations contributed the highest share of sales.
* Tier 2 locations followed closely behind.
* Tier 1 locations generated comparatively lower sales.

### Outlet Performance

* Small-sized outlets achieved the highest sales.
* Grocery Stores contributed significantly to total revenue.

### Customer Satisfaction

* The average customer rating was approximately **3.92**, indicating positive customer feedback.

## ✅ Final Conclusion

The BlinkIT Grocery Sales Analysis Dashboard successfully transforms raw retail data into actionable business insights. The analysis highlights that **Fruits & Vegetables** and **Snack Foods** are the major revenue contributors, while **Tier 3 locations** generate the highest sales. Additionally, **small-sized outlets** demonstrate strong performance, indicating potential opportunities for future expansion.

By leveraging Power BI's powerful visualization and analytical capabilities, this dashboard provides stakeholders with a comprehensive view of business performance, enabling smarter decisions and driving operational excellence.
